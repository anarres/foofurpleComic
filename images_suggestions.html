Moar images!

Want more images? Here's a few ways you could get some:

If one of the existing images is almost right, go ahead and tweak it to fit your needs. If you end up sharing your image be sure to give the artist credit and use a compatible license (if the original image has a Share-Alike license, derivative works of it have to be Share-Alike too).

A huge number of public domain clip-arts are available at Open Clipart Library and Clker.

Some possible sources of Creative Commons licensed artworks are:

  • Flickr: Creative Commons
  • Deviantart contains a lot of beautiful artwork, and some users add a Creative Commons license to their work.
  • Creative Commons search

Note that images with a "No Derivatives" license can't be used on Foofurple Comics.

You could always make your own images using a drawing program such as Inkscape or the Gimp.

Or you could have fun playing with one of the online drawing tools listed here: 10 best HTML5 sketching and drawing tools for designers.
